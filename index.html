AI voice doesn’t fail quietly. It fails publicly.
Most systems review output after the damage is done. VoiceGuard blocks unsafe actions before they reach customers — and proves it with decision logs.
Prevent

Prove

Reduce exposure
Less legal/reputational risk from one bad call.
Three steps. No magic.
1) Ask
Your agent asks VoiceGuard: “May I do this action?”
2) Decide
VoiceGuard evaluates policy and returns ALLOW or DENY.
3) Prove
Every decision is logged for audit and incident review.
