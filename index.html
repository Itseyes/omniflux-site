What OmniFlux is building

OmniFlux Digital owns and develops all products in-house. Each product runs on the same agent platform, AgenticOS, giving us one core engine and multiple revenue lines.
Core Platform · Internal infrastructure

AgenticOS – OmniFlux Agent Platform

The internal agent platform that powers every OmniFlux product. It connects language models, video generators, audio tools, and posting APIs into durable, monitored workflows.

  • Job queues, retries, and lifecycle tracking.
  • Agent workflows for scripting, QA, and publishing.
  • Central place to plug in new models and channels.
Agent orchestration Backend platform
Product · Internal pilot

Omega Forge – Video Factory for Trades

AI-powered engine that turns local trades (electricians, plumbers, gardeners etc.) into consistent TikTok/Shorts content using cinematic templates and Veo 3.

  • Job console for video generation & posting.
  • Trade-specific cinematic “bibles” and seasonal packs.
  • Fail-soft design with placeholder videos if APIs fail.
Veo 3 TikTok API
Product · In development

FlowTwin – AI Spokesperson Engine

Learns a creator or brand’s tone, then generates scripts and spokesperson-style content on demand. Built for content creators, educators, and agencies.

  • Onboarding to capture voice, style, and niche.
  • Idea generation, script drafting, and iteration.
  • Voice and delivery via tools like ElevenLabs.

OmniFlux is founder-led and currently focused on hardening AgenticOS, piloting Omega Forge, and building FlowTwin into a usable beta.

AgenticOS as the core
All products share the same AgenticOS core, so improvements to workflow orchestration, logging, or reliability benefit the entire OmniFlux stack.
2
Products as focused layers
Omega Forge and FlowTwin specialize this core for two clear use-cases: trades-based short-form video and spokesperson-style content generation.
3
Iterate with real usage
Early pilots and internal tests feed back into AgenticOS. This loop helps us turn experiments into a stable, scalable product line under the OmniFlux banner.
